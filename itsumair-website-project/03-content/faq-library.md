## Pricing and commercial

**Q1. How much does this cost?**
Answer directly with the starting figure and link to pricing. Never deflect.

**Q2. How long until I see results?**
Be honest: GBP improvements can show in weeks, competitive local rankings typically take 3–6 months. Never promise faster. Honesty here builds more trust than optimism.

**Q3. Do I have to sign a long contract?**
The #1 objection in contractor marketing. Answer with the actual terms.

**Q4. What if it doesn't work?**
Explain what happens, what is reviewed, and how to exit.

**Q5. Can I cancel?**
Plain terms, no fine print language.

**Q6. Do you require ad spend on top?**
No, and explain the asset-versus-rented-traffic position.

**Q13. Do you guarantee rankings?**

## Google Business Profile

**Q8. Do I own my Google Business Profile?**
Yes, always. Explain ownership and access.

**Q9. How many reviews do I need?**
Honest, competitive framing relative to local competitors.

**Q10. Can you get me reviews?**

**Q11. What if my profile is suspended?**

## Website

**Q15. I already have a website. Do I need a new one?**
Not necessarily. Explain when to fix versus rebuild. Recommending against a sale builds trust.

**Q16. Do I own the website?**
Yes. Explain hosting, code, and domain ownership plainly. Big objection for burned contractors.

**Q17. Can I edit it myself?**
Answer honestly based on the stack chosen.

**Q18. How long does a website take?**
Realistic timeline.

**Q19. What does maintenance actually include?**
Concrete list, not vague "ongoing support."

**Q20. What happens if my site gets hacked?**
Umair has genuine malware remediation experience. Use it.

**Q21. Do I need maintenance if my site is new?**
Honest answer.

## Working together

**Q12. You're not based in Tampa. How does that work?** ← MUST be on the homepage
Answer directly and confidently: how communication works, US phone number, response times, and the fact that local SEO is executed remotely by agencies everywhere. Do not dodge. Prospects who wonder and get no answer do not convert.

**Q22. Who will I actually talk to?**
Umair, always. No account managers. This is the advantage of a solo specialist.

**Q26. What time zone are you in and when can we talk?**
State available hours in Eastern Time. Concrete.

**Q27. How do you report progress?**
Monthly, plain English, with a dashboard. Mention the dashboard.
