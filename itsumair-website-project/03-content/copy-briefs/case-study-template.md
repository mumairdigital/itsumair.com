# Copy Brief: Case Study Template

**URL:** `/results/[client-slug]/`
**Word count:** 800–1,200 each
**Priority:** Case study 1 is P0

## Integrity rules — read before writing
- Every number real and verifiable
- Timeframe always stated (results without timeframe read as inflated)
- If the client cannot be named, use "a Tampa bathroom remodeler" and say why
- **Adjacent or pre-niche work must be clearly labeled as related work**, never presented as bathroom-remodeler marketing results
- Written client permission secured before publishing

---

## Structure

### 1. Hero
- H1: outcome-led, e.g. `How [Client] Went From Page 3 to the Map Pack in 5 Months`
- Headline metric displayed large
- Hero image

### 2. Client snapshot
Compact card: business type, market, size, services used, engagement length

### 3. The problem
- H2: `The Problem`
- What was broken, with data
- What it was costing them

### 4. What the audit found
- H2: `What the Audit Found`
- Specific bulleted findings
- **Demonstrates expertise more than any other section.** Technical enough to be credible, plain enough to read.

### 5. What I did
- H2: `What I Did`
- Organized by workstream
- Before and after screenshots

### 6. Results
- H2: `The Results`
- Before → after table or metric blocks
- Timeframe stated
- Chart where data supports it

### 7. Client quote
Full name, company, photo

### 8. Services used
Links to relevant service pages

### 9. CTA block
