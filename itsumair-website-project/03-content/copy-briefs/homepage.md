## H1 options (Decision D4 — pick one)

**Option A — Direct/clear (recommended)**
`Local SEO for Bathroom Remodeling Companies in Tampa Bay`
Sub: `I help bathroom remodelers get found by homeowners ready to buy, by fixing the website, the Google listing, and the local search system underneath.`

**Option B — Problem-led**
`Homeowners in Tampa Are Searching for Bathroom Remodelers. Are They Finding You?`
Sub: `Local SEO and websites built for one type of business: bathroom remodeling companies in Tampa Bay.`

**Option C — Brand-line-led**
`I Don't Just Rank You. I Build the System Behind It.`
Sub: `Local SEO and website design for bathroom remodeling companies in Tampa Bay.`

**Recommendation:** A. Option C is the strongest brand line but buries the niche below the fold in scanning terms. If C is chosen, the subhead must carry the niche and market with equal visual weight.

## Section-by-section brief

### 1. Hero
- H1 per above
- Subhead: value proposition, 1–2 sentences
- Primary CTA: "Get My Free Visibility Audit"
- Secondary: "See Results"
- Micro-trust: "Free. No obligation. 24-hour turnaround."

### 2. Trust bar
Real client logos OR 3 stat blocks. If neither exists, omit. Never fake.

### 3. Problem
- H2: `You're Great at Bathrooms. But Are Homeowners Finding You?`
- Intro: 2–3 sentences naming his reality
- 3 cards:
  - **You're invisible on Google Maps** — homeowners searching right now see your competitor
  - **You're renting leads** — Angi and HomeAdvisor sell the same lead to three other companies
  - **Your website doesn't sell** — traffic arrives, nobody calls

### 4. Solution / process
- H2: `How I Get Bathroom Remodelers Into the Map Pack`
- 4 steps: Audit → Fix → Build → Report
- One sentence each, plain language
- Inline CTA

### 5. Services
- H2: `What I Do`
- 4 cards, one sentence each, linking to service pages

### 6. Proof
- H2: `Real Results for Real Remodelers`
- 2–3 case study cards with a headline metric each
- CTA: "See All Case Studies"
- **If no Tampa results exist, label adjacent work honestly**

### 7. Why specialist
- H2: `Why I Only Work With Bathroom Remodelers`
- 3–4 short paragraphs
- Must include the tile and bathroom showroom background
- Key line: he understands the product, not just the marketing

### 8. Founder
- H2: `Hi, I'm Umair`
- 2–3 short first-person paragraphs
- Headshot
- CTA: "More About Me"

### 9. Testimonials
- H2: `What Clients Say`
- 2–3, full names and companies. Omit if none exist.

### 10. Service areas
- H2: `Serving Bathroom Remodelers Across Tampa Bay`
- Short paragraph + 6 location links

### 11. FAQ
- H2: `Questions Remodelers Ask Me`
- 5–7 items. **Must include:** cost, timeline, contract, "you're not in Tampa."

### 12. Final CTA
- H2: `Let's Find Out Why Homeowners Aren't Calling You`
- Supporting line, primary CTA, phone and email
