## Sections

### 1. Hero
- H1
- Subhead with the response promise: "I reply within one business day."

### 2. Two-path layout
**Left — form (5 fields max)**
Name, business name, website, phone or email, biggest problem (dropdown)
Privacy line near submit.

**Right — direct contact**
- Email
- US phone, click-to-call
- WhatsApp (presented as an option, not the default)
- Booking link
- Available hours **stated in Eastern Time** — critical for a US audience
- Service area statement

### 3. Booking
- H2: `Book a 20-Minute Visibility Review`
- Calendar embed with timezone auto-detection
- Set expectations: no pitch, just a look at your current visibility

### 4. What happens next
- H2
- 3 steps with timing

### 5. Short FAQ
3 items maximum: response time, what the call covers, whether it costs anything
