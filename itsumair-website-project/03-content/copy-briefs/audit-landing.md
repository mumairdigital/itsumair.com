# Copy Brief: Audit Landing Page

**URL:** `/audit/`
**Word count:** 600–900
**Priority:** P0

## Purpose
The primary conversion asset. This URL goes in every cold email, DM, and social bio. Reduced navigation, single purpose.

---

## H1
`Free Visibility Audit for Tampa Bay Bathroom Remodelers`

## Sections

### 1. Hero
- H1 + subhead: what you get, how fast
- Form directly in hero on desktop
- Micro-trust: "Free. No obligation. 24-hour turnaround."

### 2. What you get
- H2: `What You'll Get`
- Be specific — vague audits do not convert:
  - Your current Map Pack position for the terms that matter
  - How your review count compares to your top 3 competitors
  - Your Google Business Profile completeness score
  - Website speed and mobile check
  - The top 3 things to fix, in priority order
- Delivered as a short PDF or video walkthrough

### 3. Sample audit preview
- H2: `Here's What It Looks Like`
- Screenshot or short video of a real audit
- **High converting. Let them see before they commit.**

### 4. What it costs
- H2: `What Does It Cost?`
- "Nothing." Then explain honestly why: it is how I start conversations, and most people who see their own numbers want to talk.
- Honesty about the motive builds more trust than pretending it is charity.

### 5. What happens next
- H2: `What Happens Next`
- 1. You fill in the form (2 minutes)
- 2. I run the audit and send it within one business day
- 3. If it is useful, we talk. If not, keep the audit.
- **"Keep the audit either way" removes the pressure that kills conversions.**

### 6. Who this is for
- H2: `Who This Is For`
- Bathroom remodeling companies in Tampa Bay, already operating
- Qualifying language filters bad leads

### 8. FAQ
Q23, Q24, Q25
