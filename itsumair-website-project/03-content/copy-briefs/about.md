- Direct, plain reporting

- H2: `What I Believe`
- Truth over theatre: real proof only. Reference the practice of replacing fabricated testimonials with genuine ones.
- Systems over quick wins
- Fix the root cause, not the symptom
- Short. Values, not a sermon.

### 5. Background and credentials
- H2: `Background`
- Real technical work: malware infection traced to a database trigger and remediated, indexation recovery, full site rebuilds
- Certifications and tools
- Concrete beats impressive-sounding

- H2: `Working With Me From Overseas`

- How communication works: US phone number, email, WhatsApp, scheduled calls in Eastern Time
