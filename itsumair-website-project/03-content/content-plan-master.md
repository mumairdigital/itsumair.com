# Master Content Plan

## Governing rule
Every word on this site is written for a bathroom remodeling business owner in Tampa Bay. Never a homeowner.

## Content production order
Write in this sequence — each page informs the next.

1. **Homepage** — establishes voice and core messaging
2. **About** — the story everything else references
3. **4 service pages** — the offer
4. **Audit landing page** — the conversion asset
5. **Contact**
6. **Pillar guide** — the big one, write when voice is established
7. **Pricing**
8. **Case studies**
9. **Location pages**
10. **Blog posts**
11. **Legal pages**

## Blog strategy

**Audience: remodeling business owners. Never homeowners.**
Test for every post: would a remodeler forward this to another remodeler?

### Four content pillars
1. **Get found** — Map Pack, GBP, reviews, local rankings
2. **Convert more** — website, quote process, follow-up speed
3. **Business of remodeling** — lead costs, escaping shared-lead platforms, pricing, seasonality
4. **Teardowns and data** — anonymized audits of real Tampa remodeler visibility, local market analysis. Most shareable and most credibility-building format.

### Launch posts (first 3, build the rest post-launch)
| # | Title | Pillar | Target keyword |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Why Bathroom Remodelers Don't Show Up in the Google Map Pack | Get found | bathroom remodeler google maps |
| 2 | What a Bathroom Remodeling Lead Actually Costs in Tampa | Business | bathroom remodeling leads cost |

### Cadence
Per Decision D8. Recommendation: 2 per month, sustained. Consistency beats volume. Ten excellent posts outperform fifty thin ones in a low-volume niche.

- No page published that could apply equally to a dentist (specificity test)
