# Legal Pages Brief

**Not legal advice.** These are content requirements. Have final wording reviewed by a qualified professional, especially given US-facing data collection.

---

## Privacy Policy (`/privacy-policy/`)

Required because the site collects personal data from US residents via forms.

**Must cover:**
- What data is collected (name, business, email, phone, website, message; analytics data)
- How it is collected (forms, cookies, analytics)
- Why it is collected (to respond to enquiries, deliver audits, improve the site)
- Who it is shared with (analytics providers, email provider, booking tool — name them)
- How long it is kept
- International transfer note (data processed outside the US — relevant given Umair's location, and material for US visitors)
- User rights, including California residents (CCPA/CPRA): right to know, delete, opt out of sale (state clearly that data is not sold)
- Cookie usage
- How to contact regarding data
- Last updated date

## Terms of Service (`/terms/`)

**Must cover:**
- Who operates the site
- Acceptable use
- Intellectual property (site content ownership)
- Disclaimer: no guarantee of specific search rankings or business results — important given the industry
- Limitation of liability
- Third-party links disclaimer
- Governing law
- Changes to terms
- Contact

## Accessibility Statement (`/accessibility/`)

**Recommended and not in the original brief.** US service-business websites receive accessibility demand letters; a published statement plus genuine WCAG 2.1 AA compliance materially reduces exposure and is the right thing to do for a 45–65 audience.

**Must cover:**
- Commitment to accessibility
- Standard targeted (WCAG 2.1 Level AA)
- Measures taken
- Known limitations, if any
- How to report an accessibility problem, with a real contact method and response commitment
- Last reviewed date

---

## Cookie consent
If analytics cookies are used, show a consent notice. Keep it simple and non-intrusive. Do not use a dark-pattern banner; this audience distrusts tricks.

## Form disclosure
Every form must include a short line near the submit button linking to the privacy policy.

## Checklist
- [ ] Privacy policy written and reviewed
- [ ] Terms written and reviewed
- [ ] Accessibility statement written
- [ ] Cookie notice implemented
- [ ] All three linked in the footer
- [ ] Privacy linked from every form
- [ ] Last-updated dates present
