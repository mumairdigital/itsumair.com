# Location Brief: St. Petersburg

**URL:** `/areas/st-petersburg/` | **Priority:** P1

## Why this city is included
The metro's second core city. Large stock of mid-century homes with original bathrooms, creating strong renovation need. Substantial base of remodeling companies. Distinct market character from Tampa, with its own competitive set.

## Local angles to research and use
- Old Northeast: historic homes, affluent, period properties with original bathrooms
- Mid-century housing stock across central St. Pete now well past bathroom replacement age
- Downtown St. Petersburg: condo renovation demand
- Snell Isle: high-value waterfront properties
- 55+ condo communities including large developments in the western part of the city
