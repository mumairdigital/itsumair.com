# Location Brief: Clearwater

**URL:** `/areas/clearwater/` | **Priority:** P1

## Why this city is included
**The strongest aging-in-place market in the metro, and the single best fit for the niche wedge.**

Clearwater contains very large 55+ communities. On Top of the World Clearwater alone comprises roughly 4,959 condominium homes and is one of the largest such communities in the Tampa Bay area. Terrace Park of Five Towns was built between 1972 and 1987. This is exactly the profile that drives walk-in shower conversions and accessible bathroom remodels: older residents in homes with bathrooms that are 40+ years old.

## Local angles to research and use
- On Top of the World Clearwater — approximately 4,959 homes, one of the largest communities in Tampa Bay
- Terrace Park of Five Towns — built 1972 to 1987, original-era bathrooms
- Regency Heights and other 55+ communities
- Condo and villa stock with 1970s and 1980s bathrooms
- Aging-in-place and accessibility demand: walk-in showers, grab bars, curbless entries
