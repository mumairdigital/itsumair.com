# Location Brief: Tampa

**URL:** `/areas/tampa/` | **Priority:** P1

## Why this city is included
The anchor city and largest concentration of target businesses in Hillsborough County. Affluent, older housing in South Tampa, Hyde Park, Palma Ceia, and Seminole Heights supports high-value bathroom remodel demand. The densest supply of bathroom remodeling companies in the metro, meaning the largest prospect pool.

## Local angles to research and use
- Historic Hyde Park: stately older homes, affluent, original bathrooms in period properties
- Palma Ceia and South Tampa: high incomes, older housing stock, high remodel ticket values
- Seminole Heights: bungalow stock, active renovation culture
- Carrollwood: established affluent north Tampa with mature homes
- Davis Islands, Harbour Island: high-value waterfront properties
