# Location Brief: Brandon

**URL:** `/areas/brandon/` | **Priority:** P2

## Why this city is included
Major Hillsborough suburb east of Tampa. A fast-growing residential district with mature, low-crime neighborhoods and predominantly single-family detached housing. Homes from the 1980s and 1990s are now reaching bathroom replacement age. Large homeowner base with a solid count of local remodelers, and less saturated than Tampa proper.

## Local angles to research and use
- Suburban single-family stock, largely 1980s to 1990s build era
- Median home price historically below the Florida average, so mid-range remodels rather than luxury
- Family-oriented, established neighborhoods with long-tenure homeowners
- Growth corridor along the Selmon Expressway toward Tampa
- Homeowners more likely to renovate than relocate given rate lock-in
