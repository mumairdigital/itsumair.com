# Keyword Map

## Governing principle
**Every keyword targets bathroom remodeling business owners. Never homeowners.**

| Keyword | Searcher | Target? |
|---|---|---|
| bathroom remodeling tampa | Homeowner | NO |
| bathroom remodel cost | Homeowner | NO |
| walk in shower installation tampa | Homeowner | NO |
| bathroom remodeler seo | Business owner | YES |
| marketing for bathroom remodelers | Business owner | YES |
| how to get bathroom remodeling leads | Business owner | YES |

## Expectation setting
Buyer-intent keywords in this niche have very low volume, likely tens of searches per month. Ranking for all of them might produce a few hundred visits monthly. **That is expected and acceptable** because those visitors are exactly the right people. The site converts outreach traffic; SEO is the long-tail bonus.

## Validation before launch
- [ ] Run all primary keywords through Ahrefs or Semrush for real volume
- [ ] Check SERPs manually — confirm results are business-facing, not homeowner-facing
- [ ] Identify which terms already have niche agency competitors ranking
- [ ] Adjust targets based on real data, not assumptions

**If a keyword's SERP is dominated by homeowner-facing results, do not target it.** The SERP tells you the intent Google has assigned.
