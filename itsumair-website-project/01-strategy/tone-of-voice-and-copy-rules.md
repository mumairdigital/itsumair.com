# Tone of Voice and Copy Rules

Apply these to every word on the site. QA against this file before launch.

## Core rules

1. **8th grade reading level.** Test it. If a sentence needs re-reading, rewrite it.
2. **Short sentences.** Average under 20 words. Vary length so it does not read robotically.
3. **Second person.** Talk to the reader as "you." Umair is "I," never "we."
4. **Concrete over abstract.** "You show up 7th on Google Maps" beats "suboptimal local visibility."
5. **Numbers wherever possible.** Specific numbers are the cheapest credibility available.
6. **No em dashes.** House style (consistent with Umair's existing content guidelines). Use commas, colons, or full stops.
7. **US English spelling.** Optimize, color, center, analyze.
8. **Active voice.**
9. **No jargon.** If a remodeler would not say it on a job site, do not write it.

## Banned words and phrases
leverage, synergy, solutions, cutting-edge, best-in-class, game-changer, seamless, robust, holistic, ecosystem, unlock, elevate, supercharge, revolutionize, "in today's digital landscape," "it's no secret that," "we're passionate about"

## Banned claims
- Guaranteed rankings or guaranteed lead volume
- "#1 on Google"
- Any team language ("our team," "our experts")
- Any implication of US physical presence
- Any client, result, or testimonial that is not real

## Preferred constructions

| Instead of | Write |
|---|---|
| We provide comprehensive local SEO solutions | I get bathroom remodelers into the Google Map Pack |
| Utilize | Use |
| In order to | To |
| At this point in time | Now |
| Prior to | Before |
| Assist | Help |
| Commence | Start |
| Our team of experts | I |

## Headline rules
- Lead with the outcome or the problem, not the service name
- Include the niche or market where natural
- Under 12 words where possible
- Never use a headline that could apply to any business in any industry

## CTA copy rules
- First person, action oriented: "Get My Free Audit," not "Submit" or "Learn More"
- Say what happens next: "Book a 20-Minute Visibility Review"
- Never use "Click Here"

## Structure rules
- Front-load the answer. Contractors scan, they do not read.
- One idea per paragraph, maximum 3 sentences
- Use bullets for anything list-shaped
- Bold the single most important sentence in a long section
- Every page ends with a CTA

## The credibility test
Before publishing any page, ask: would a skeptical 55-year-old Tampa contractor who has been burned by an agency believe this? If any sentence sounds like marketing rather than a person talking, rewrite it.

## The specificity test
Search the page for any sentence that would still be true if you swapped "bathroom remodeler" for "dentist." Rewrite it. Generic sentences are wasted space on a specialist site.
