# Conversion Strategy

## The single conversion goal
**Free Tampa Bay bathroom remodeler visibility audit request.**

One offer, repeated everywhere. Competing offers reduce conversions.

## Conversion hierarchy
| Priority | Action | Where |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Request free audit | Every page |
| 2 | Book a 20-minute call | Contact, audit page, after proof sections |
| 3 | Call directly | Mobile sticky bar, header |
| 4 | View case studies | Homepage, service pages |
| 5 | Read blog / pillar | Nav, internal links |

## Why the audit offer works for this audience
- Free and concrete — he can see the value before spending
- It demonstrates competence rather than claiming it
- It surfaces a problem he did not know he had
- It creates a natural reason for a follow-up call
- It is exactly what he would pay for later

**Audit must include:** current Map Pack position for core terms, review count versus top 3 competitors, GBP completeness score, website speed and mobile check, top 3 fixes. Deliverable: short PDF or video walkthrough.

## CTA placement rules
- Hero (above fold)
- Immediately after the problem section
- Immediately after proof sections
- End of every page
- Sticky header button
- Sticky mobile bar (call + audit)
- Never two competing primary CTAs in one viewport

## Trust elements ranked by impact on this audience
1. Real case studies with numbers
2. Video testimonials from actual clients
3. Umair's face and name throughout
4. Specificity — "bathroom remodelers in Tampa Bay" beats "home service businesses"
5. Published pricing — rare, disarming, filters time-wasters
6. "You own everything" guarantee
7. No long-term contracts
8. Written testimonials with full names, companies, photos
9. Certifications
10. Response-time promise, honored
11. Transparent process — what happens in months 1, 2, 3

## Friction removal
- Forms maximum 5 fields
- Click-to-call everywhere on mobile
- No account creation, no downloads gated behind long forms
- Booking with timezone auto-detection
- Response time stated and honored
- Answer price and contract questions before he asks

## Objection handling map
| Objection | Where it is answered |
|---|---|
| How much | Pricing page, pricing signal on every service page |
| How long | Homepage FAQ, service FAQs, process section |
| Contract | Homepage FAQ, pricing page |
| Different from last agency | Positioning section, case studies |
| Not in Tampa | About page + FAQ, answered directly |
| Do I own it | FAQ, pricing page |
| Already have a website | Website design service page |
| How do I know it works | Process section, reporting mention, analytics dashboard demo |

## Qualifying (deliberate friction)
Include a short "who I am not a fit for" section. Filtering improves close rate and protects time. Example criteria: businesses outside Tampa Bay, businesses not doing bathroom remodels, anyone wanting guaranteed rankings, anyone wanting the cheapest option.

## Measurement
Track as GA4 conversion events: audit form submit, contact form submit, booking completed, phone click, email click, scroll to 75% on pillar and case study pages.
