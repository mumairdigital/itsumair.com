# Brand Guidelines

## Brand identity
- **Name:** Muhammad Umair (display: Umair)
- **Domain:** itsumair.com
- **Brand line:** "I Don't Just Rank You. I Build the System Behind It."
- **Niche qualifier (must appear near the brand line):** Local SEO for Bathroom Remodeling Companies in Tampa Bay

## Brand personality
Straight-talking, competent, calm under a mess. The person you call when something is broken and nobody else can explain why. Low drama, high signal.

## Brand values
1. **Truth over theatre** — real proof only
2. **Systems over quick wins**
3. **Ownership** — fix the root cause, not the symptom

## Color

**Primary: #6700C8**

Critical constraint — this is a saturated violet:
- White text on #6700C8 background: passes WCAG AA. Use for buttons and dark sections.
- #6700C8 text on white background: does NOT pass AA at small sizes. Use only for large headings (24px+ bold) or decorative elements.
- Body text must be near-black, never violet.

**Recommended role assignment**
| Role | Usage |
|---|---|
| Primary #6700C8 | Primary CTAs, key section backgrounds, large headings, accents |
| Text | Near-black (approx #14121A) for body |
| Background | Warm off-white (approx #FAF9FB) |
| Surface | White for cards |
| Border | Light neutral |
| Success/proof | A distinct accent for results and metrics, so proof reads differently from brand |

Exact values to be finalized in 05-design/design-tokens-spec.md.

**Audience constraint:** users are 45–65, on phones, in Florida daylight. High contrast is a conversion requirement, not just compliance. Never use low-contrast trendy styling.

## Typography direction
- One primary typeface, clear and modern, excellent legibility at small sizes
- Body minimum 16px, ideally 17–18px for this audience
- Generous line height (1.6+) for readability
- Avoid thin weights entirely

## Logo usage
- SVG, light and dark variants
- Clear space equal to the height of the mark on all sides
- Never stretch, recolor outside brand palette, or place on low-contrast backgrounds

## Photography style
- Real over stock wherever possible
- Real screenshots and real data are the highest-credibility visuals available
- Consistent crop ratios and a subtle consistent color treatment
- Never use client or competitor photography without written permission

## Voice attributes
