# Internal Linking Map

## Architecture: hub and spoke
```
Blog posts ──► Pillar page ──► Service pages ──► Audit / Contact
                    ▲                ▲
Location pages ─────┘                │
Case studies ────────────────────────┘
```

## Rules
1. Contextual, descriptive anchor text. Never "click here" or "read more" as the only anchor.
2. Links exist because they help the reader, not to hit a count.
3. No orphan pages. Everything reachable within 3 clicks.
4. Every page links to at least one conversion page.
5. Do not link the same anchor to different destinations across the site.

## Per-page outbound link requirements

**Homepage** → all 4 service pages, results hub, about, pillar, 6 location pages (in service area section), audit

**Service pages** → pillar, 2 related service pages, 1 relevant case study, pricing, audit, 1–2 location pages

**Pillar page** → all 4 service pages, 2–3 case studies, relevant blog posts, audit. This page should be the most internally linked-to page on the site.

**Case studies** → the service used, pillar, audit, results hub

**Location pages** → 2–3 nearby location pages, 2 relevant service pages, 1 case study, audit

**Blog posts** → pillar (always), 1 relevant service page, 1 case study where relevant, audit

**About** → results hub, services hub, contact, audit

**Pricing** → all 4 service pages, audit, FAQ anchor

**Audit** → minimal outbound links. This is a conversion page; do not give people exits.

## Anchor text guidance

| Destination | Preferred anchors |
|---|---|
| `/services/local-seo/` | "local SEO for bathroom remodelers", "get into the Map Pack" |
| `/services/google-business-profile/` | "Google Business Profile optimization", "optimize your Google listing" |
| `/services/website-design/` | "bathroom remodeler website design", "a website that converts" |
| `/services/website-maintenance/` | "website maintenance", "keep your site secure and fast" |
| `/bathroom-remodeler-marketing/` | "complete guide to marketing a bathroom remodeling business" |
| `/audit/` | "free visibility audit", "get your free audit" |

Vary anchors naturally. Do not use the identical anchor on every instance.

## Location cross-linking pattern
| Page | Links to |
|---|---|
| Tampa | Brandon, St. Petersburg |
| St. Petersburg | Clearwater, Largo, Tampa |
| Clearwater | Largo, Palm Harbor, St. Petersburg |
| Largo | Clearwater, St. Petersburg, Palm Harbor |
| Brandon | Tampa, Riverview (post-launch) |
| Palm Harbor | Clearwater, Largo |

Link geographic neighbors only. Linking Brandon to Palm Harbor makes no sense to a reader and no sense to Google.
