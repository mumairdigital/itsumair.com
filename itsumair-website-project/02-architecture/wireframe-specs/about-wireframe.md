# About Page Wireframe Specification

The highest-trust page on the site. Remodelers hire people, not logos.

---

## Section 1 — Hero
- H1: "I'm Umair. I Build Search Systems for Bathroom Remodelers."
- Subhead: one sentence positioning
- Primary headshot, large

## Section 2 — Why bathrooms
- H2: "Why I Only Work With Bathroom Remodelers"
- **The most persuasive content on the entire site.** The tile and bathroom showroom background: product taxonomies, tile calculators, showroom e-commerce, bathroom product UX. He understands the product, not just the marketing.
- 3–4 paragraphs, first person, story format

## Section 3 — How I work
- H2: "How I Work"
- Founder-led, direct access, no account managers, no jargon
- 3–4 bullets or short paragraphs
- Never imply a team

## Section 4 — What I believe
- H2: "What I Believe"
- Truth over theatre — real proof only
- Systems over quick wins
- Fix the root cause
- Short, values-driven, no preaching

## Section 5 — Credentials and proof
- H2: "Background"
- Certifications, tools, notable technical work
- Real examples: malware remediation traced to a database trigger, indexation recovery, Next.js rebuilds

## Section 6 — Working with a specialist overseas
- H2: address it directly, e.g. "Working With Me From Overseas"
- **Do not hide this.** Answer plainly: how communication works, response times, US phone number, why local SEO is executed remotely by everyone including local agencies
- Addressing it openly converts far better than letting prospects wonder

## Section 7 — Personal note (optional, short)
- One short human paragraph. Based in Islamabad, working with US clients. Keep it brief and confident.

## Section 8 — CTA block

---

## Length target
800–1,200 words.

## Image requirements
- Primary headshot (large, hero)
- 1–2 candid or working photos
- Optional: photos from the tile/showroom background — these visually prove the "why bathrooms" story
