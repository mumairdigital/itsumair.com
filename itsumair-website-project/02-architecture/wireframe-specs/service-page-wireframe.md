# Service Page Wireframe Specification

Applies to all 4 service pages. Same skeleton, different substance.

---

## Section 1 — Hero
- Breadcrumb: Home > Services > [Service]
- H1: `[Service] for Bathroom Remodeling Companies`
- Subhead: the outcome this service produces, 1–2 sentences
- Primary CTA
- Supporting image or graphic

## Section 2 — Problem framing
- H2: the specific pain this service solves
- 2–3 short paragraphs or 3 bullets
- Must be bathroom-remodeler specific, not generic

## Section 3 — What's included
- H2: "What's Included"
- Concrete bulleted deliverables, 6–10 items
- Specificity is the selling point here. "Monthly GBP posts" beats "ongoing optimization."

## Section 4 — Process
- H2: "How It Works"
- 3–5 numbered steps
- Each: title + 1–2 sentences
- Include a timeline expectation where honest

## Section 5 — Proof
- H2: results relevant to THIS service
- 1 case study card or a before/after data block
- Link to full case study

## Section 6 — Who it's for / not for
- H2: "Who This Is For"
- Two columns or two lists: good fit / not a fit
- Deliberate qualifying. Builds trust and filters bad leads.

## Section 7 — Pricing signal
- H2 or inline block
- "Starts at $X/month" or "Projects start at $X"
- Link to /pricing/
- Never hide price entirely on a service page

## Section 8 — FAQ
- H2: "Common Questions"
- 4–6 service-specific Q&As, accordion
- Sourced from 03-content/faq-library.md

## Section 9 — Related services
- H2: "Works Well With"
- 2 cards linking to complementary services
- Local SEO ↔ GBP; Website Design ↔ Website Maintenance

## Section 10 — CTA block
- Full-width, brand background
- Heading, supporting line, primary CTA, phone

---

## Length target
1,200–1,800 words. Depth signals expertise to a skeptical buyer.

## Per-service emphasis
| Service | Lead angle |
|---|---|
| Local SEO | Map Pack rankings, service-area strategy, citations |
| GBP | Fastest visible win. Reviews, photos, posts, categories. Easiest first sale. |
| Website Design | Conversion first. Warmest sell given Umair's showroom background. |
| Website Maintenance | Protection and peace of mind, not a commodity |
