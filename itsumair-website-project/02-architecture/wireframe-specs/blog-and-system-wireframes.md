# Blog, Hub, and System Page Wireframes

---

# BLOG HUB (`/blog/`)

- H1: "Marketing Advice for Bathroom Remodelers"
- Short intro: who this is for (business owners, not homeowners)
- Category filter: Get Found / Convert More / Business of Remodeling / Teardowns
- Post grid: featured image, title, excerpt, category, read time
- Pagination
- Sidebar or footer CTA: audit

---

# BLOG POST TEMPLATE

- Breadcrumb: Home > Blog > [Post]
- H1
- Meta line: author (Umair, with headshot), date, read time
- Featured image
- Optional: key takeaways box at top (contractors scan)
- Body with H2/H3 structure
- In-content CTA after roughly 50% scroll
- Author bio box with headshot and link to About
- Related posts (3)
- CTA block

**Requirements:** every post links to the pillar page. Every post targets business owners, never homeowners.

---

# SERVICES HUB (`/services/`)

- H1: "Services for Bathroom Remodeling Companies"
- Intro: 2–3 sentences
- 4 service cards, each: icon, name, 2 sentences, what's included preview, link
- "Not sure what you need?" section pointing to the audit
- CTA block

---

# RESULTS HUB (`/results/`)

- H1: "Results"
- Intro: short, honest framing
- Case study card grid: image, client type, headline metric, link
- CTA block

---

# AREAS HUB (`/areas/`)

- H1: "Serving Bathroom Remodelers Across Tampa Bay"
- Intro: the Tampa Bay market and why it matters for bathroom remodeling
- Grid of 6 location cards, each with a one-line local hook
- Optional branded map graphic
- CTA block

---

# 404 PAGE

- H1: "That Page Doesn't Exist"
- Short friendly line, no jargon or jokes that need explaining
- Links: Home, Services, Results, Contact
- Primary CTA
- Search if available
