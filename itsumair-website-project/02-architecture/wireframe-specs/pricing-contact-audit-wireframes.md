# Pricing, Contact, and Audit Page Wireframes

---

# PRICING PAGE

## Rationale
Contractors quote jobs for a living. They respect published pricing and read opacity as a trick. Publishing also filters tire-kickers.

## Sections

**1. Hero**
- H1: "Straightforward Pricing. No Long Contracts."
- Subhead

**2. Why I publish pricing**
- Short honest paragraph. This paragraph itself builds trust.

**3. Tiers**
- 3 cards: Foundation / Growth / Full System
- Each: "starting at" monthly price, who it is for, what is included, CTA
- Middle tier visually emphasized as recommended

**4. Website design pricing**
- Project-based "starting at" range
- What affects the price

**5. What affects price**
- Honest variables: number of locations, current site condition, competition level, review volume

**6. What's NOT included**
- Integrity signal. Ad spend, third-party tools, content beyond X, etc.

**7. ROI framing** ← give this real estate
- One $15k bathroom job versus a year of retainer
- Simple, concrete math
- This is the strongest argument on the page

**8. Pricing FAQ**
- Contract length, cancellation, ownership, payment terms, price increases

**9. CTA block**

**Length:** 700–1,000 words

# CONTACT PAGE

## Sections

**1. Hero**
- H1: "Let's Talk"
- Subhead with response-time promise

**2. Two-path layout**
- Left: form (5 fields max)
  - Name, business name, website, phone or email, "biggest problem right now" (dropdown)
- Right: direct contact
  - Email, US phone (click-to-call), WhatsApp option, booking link, response time, service area

**3. Calendar embed**
- "Book a 20-Minute Visibility Review"
- Timezone auto-detection

**4. What happens next**
- 3 steps so there is no uncertainty

**5. Short FAQ**
- 3 items maximum

**Length:** 300–500 words

# AUDIT LANDING PAGE

## Purpose
The primary conversion asset. This URL goes in every cold email and DM. Minimal navigation, single purpose.

## Sections

**1. Hero**
- H1: "Free Visibility Audit for Tampa Bay Bathroom Remodelers"
- Subhead: what they get and how fast
- Form directly in hero on desktop

**2. What you get**
- Specific deliverables:
  - Current Map Pack position for your core terms
  - Review gap versus your top 3 competitors
  - Google Business Profile completeness score
  - Website speed and mobile check
  - Top 3 fixes, prioritized
- Be concrete. Vague audits do not convert.

**3. Sample audit preview**
- Screenshot or short video of an actual audit
- High converting. Let them see what they get.

**4. What it costs**
- "Nothing." Then explain why it is free honestly: it is how conversations start.

**5. What happens next**
- 3 steps, with timing

**6. Who this is for**
- Qualifying: Tampa Bay, bathroom remodeling, already in business

**7. Form (repeated)**

**8. Short FAQ**
- 3 items: is it really free, how long does it take, what do you do with my information

## Navigation
Reduced header (logo + phone only). No full nav. Do not give people exits from a conversion page.

**Length:** 600–900 words
