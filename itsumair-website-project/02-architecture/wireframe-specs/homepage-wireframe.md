# Homepage Wireframe Specification

Text wireframe. Sections in order, top to bottom. No visual design decisions here — those are in 05-design.

---

## Section 1 — Hero
**Purpose:** niche + market + outcome in 3 seconds
**Layout:** Left: headline, subhead, CTAs, trust line. Right: image or graphic. Mobile: stacked, text first.

- H1 (niche + market must be explicit)
- Subhead: 1–2 sentences, the value proposition
- Primary CTA button: "Get My Free Visibility Audit"
- Secondary CTA (text/ghost): "See Results"
- Micro trust line beneath CTAs: e.g. "Free. No obligation. 24-hour turnaround."
- Image: see 06-assets

**Requirement:** on a 375px viewport, H1 + subhead + primary CTA all visible without scrolling.

---

## Section 2 — Trust bar
**Purpose:** immediate credibility
**Layout:** horizontal strip, muted background

- Either client logos (grayscale, real only) OR 3 key stat blocks
- If no logos exist at launch, use stat blocks or omit the section entirely. Never use fake logos.

---

## Section 3 — Problem
**Purpose:** prove you understand his business
**Layout:** centered heading + 3 problem cards

- H2: problem-framed, e.g. "You're Great at Bathrooms. But Are Homeowners Finding You?"
- Intro paragraph: 2–3 sentences
- 3 cards: icon + short heading + 1–2 sentences each
  - Card 1: invisible in the Map Pack
  - Card 2: paying for shared leads
  - Card 3: website that does not convert

---

## Section 4 — Solution / How it works
**Purpose:** reduce perceived risk by showing a clear process
**Layout:** 3–4 numbered steps, horizontal on desktop, vertical on mobile

- H2: "How I Get Bathroom Remodelers Into the Map Pack"
- Steps: number + title + 1–2 sentences
  1. Audit — find what is broken
  2. Fix — website, GBP, technical
  3. Build — reviews, content, local signals
  4. Report — plain-English monthly reporting
- Inline text CTA after steps

---

## Section 5 — Services overview
**Purpose:** route to service pages
**Layout:** 4 cards, 2x2 on tablet, 1 column on mobile

- H2: "What I Do"
- 4 cards: icon + service name + 1 sentence + link
- Card order: Local SEO, Google Business Profile, Website Design, Website Maintenance

---

## Section 6 — Proof / results
**Purpose:** the conversion engine — most important section on the page
**Layout:** 2–3 case study cards with a headline metric each

- H2: "Real Results for Real Remodelers"
- Each card: image, client type, headline metric, 1 sentence, link
- CTA: "See All Case Studies"

**Constraint:** if real Tampa results do not exist at launch, use honest adjacent work clearly labeled as such. Never imply results that did not happen.

---

## Section 7 — Why a specialist
**Purpose:** the core differentiator
**Layout:** two-column, text + supporting visual

- H2: "Why I Only Work With Bathroom Remodelers"
- 3–4 short paragraphs or bullets
- Include the tile/showroom background point

---

## Section 8 — About / founder
**Purpose:** human trust
**Layout:** photo left, text right (reverse on mobile: photo first)

- H2: "Hi, I'm Umair"
- 2–3 short paragraphs, first person
- Real headshot, not an illustration
- CTA: "More About Me"

---

## Section 9 — Testimonials
**Purpose:** social proof
**Layout:** 2–3 testimonial cards or a simple slider

- H2: "What Clients Say"
- Each: quote, full name, company, photo where available
- Never anonymous. If no testimonials exist yet, omit the section rather than inventing one.

---

## Section 10 — Service areas
**Purpose:** local relevance and internal linking
**Layout:** heading + short paragraph + link grid

- H2: "Serving Bathroom Remodelers Across Tampa Bay"
- Short paragraph
- Grid of 6 location links
- Optional: simple branded map graphic

---

## Section 11 — FAQ
**Purpose:** kill final objections
**Layout:** accordion, 5–7 items

- H2: "Questions Remodelers Ask Me"
- Selected from 03-content/faq-library.md
- Must include: pricing, timeline, contract, and the "not based in Tampa" question

---

## Section 12 — Final CTA
**Purpose:** the close
**Layout:** full-width, #6700C8 background, white text, centered

- H2: e.g. "Let's Find Out Why Homeowners Aren't Calling You"
- 1–2 sentence supporting line
- Primary CTA button
- Phone number and email beneath

---

## Global elements
- Sticky header with CTA
- Sticky mobile CTA bar (call + audit)
- Footer per navigation-spec.md

## Heading hierarchy
Exactly one H1 (hero). All section titles H2. Sub-points within sections H3. Never skip levels.
