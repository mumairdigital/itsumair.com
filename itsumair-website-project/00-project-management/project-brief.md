# Project Brief

## Client
Muhammad Umair — solo specialist operating as a personal brand, based in Islamabad, Pakistan, serving US clients.

## Domain
itsumair.com (primary personal brand hub)
mumairdigital.com (services/agency layer — see decisions-log.md, Decision 9)

## Project goal
Build a premium personal brand website that attracts bathroom remodeling companies in Tampa Bay and positions Umair as a niche Local SEO specialist rather than a generic digital marketer.

## Business objectives (in priority order)
1. Convert outreach traffic into booked audit calls
2. Establish niche credibility fast enough that a skeptical contractor trusts a solo operator overseas
3. Serve as a live demonstration of the exact work being sold
4. Capture long-tail buyer search over time

## Primary services
- Local SEO
- Google Business Profile Optimization
- Website Design
- Website Maintenance

## Target customer
Owner-operators of bathroom remodeling companies in Tampa–St. Petersburg–Clearwater, FL. Revenue $300k–$3M. Age 45–65. Mobile-first. Skeptical of agencies.

## Explicitly NOT the target
Homeowners. Kitchen remodelers. General contractors. Home-service businesses outside the bathroom niche. Clients outside Tampa Bay (at launch).

## Success metrics
| Metric | Target (first 6 months) |
|---|---|
| Audit form submissions | Primary KPI |
| Booked calls | Secondary KPI |
| Signed clients | 3–5 |
| Time on key pages | > 2 min on pillar and case studies |
| Core Web Vitals | All green on mobile |
| Sessions | Explicitly NOT a primary KPI |

## Constraints
- No physical US presence (affects Google Business Profile eligibility — see 04-seo/local-seo-entity-plan.md)
- No Tampa bathroom-remodeler case studies at launch (see 03-content/testimonials-and-proof-tracker.md)
- Solo operator — the site must not promise team capacity that does not exist
- Time zone gap with US clients (PKT vs EST) — must be addressed openly, not hidden

## Non-goals
- High traffic volume
- Ranking for homeowner-intent keywords
- Serving multiple niches
- Selling paid ads management (not in service list)
